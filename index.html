The Mystery:

Nina is a kindergarten teacher, and today the children are learning about colors and shapes. She has hidden around the classroom a unique number of each of five shapes her class are to find and count. Each type of shape is in a particular color.

Can you identify which color each shape is and how many of them are hidden?

Clues:

1) The triangles are the purple shapes. There are less than 6 triangles.
2) Neither the squares nor the hexagons are green.
3) The number of circles is not 4. The circles are not red or yellow.
4) There are less octagons than there are yellow shapes.
5) There are either 6 or 7 of the brown shapes.
6) The number of yellow shapes is greater than 4.
7) The smallest number of shapes hidden in the classroom applies to the red shapes.
8) The hexagons are not the yellow shapes.
9) There are less brown shapes than green shapes.
